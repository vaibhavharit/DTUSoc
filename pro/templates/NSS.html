About Us


• National Service Scheme is to inculcate the social welfare thoughts in the students, and to provide service to the society without any prejudice.
• NSS volunteers work to ensure that every one in our society who is needy gets the every possible help from them so that they can also enhance their standards and lead a life of dignity in the society with all of us.
• To inculcate social welfare thoughts in students and undertake actions reflecting the same.
• To develop a sense of social and civic responsibility.
• To identify practical solutions to problems of the community.
