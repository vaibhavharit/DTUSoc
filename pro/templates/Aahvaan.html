The first ever sports fest. The first ever color run. The first ever social cause. Aahvaan 2017 was a revolution that transformed the infrastructure, culture and spirit of sport in DTU. Sports will take center stage again at Aahvaan 2018 with roaring crowds, thundering feet and adrenaline in the air. And to be the team behind it all: is power, is redemption, is glory. AAHVAAN is the Annual Sports Festival of Delhi Technological University to be held from 23rd to 26th March 2017. Aiming to be North India's largest sports festival, AAHVAAN will be unforgettable with its seamless mix of sport, culture and a social cause.
