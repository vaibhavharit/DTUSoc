About Us


Delhi-42: The DTU Quiz Club is the official quiz club of DTU. Founded in September 2013, we’re amongst the youngest recognized clubs/societies on campus. We are a group of like minded people who meet once a week to do what we love best, Quiz! We gained officialdom in 2013, but Delhi-42 has existed in essence for over a decade now. Quizzers from DTU (DCE) have made a mark in quizzes across Delhi-NCR and outside for a long time and continue to do so.

Delhi-42 is not a society or a club actually, it’s more of an interest group. There is no formal membership procedure, or recruitment test, or fees of any kind. We aim at improving the culture and spirit of quizzing in DTU, and anyone who wants to try their hand, is more than welcome.
